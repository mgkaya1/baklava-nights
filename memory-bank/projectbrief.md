# Project Brief - Baklava Nights

## Project Overview
Baklava Nights is an event promotion website for a Turkish Disco Pop DJ party scheduled for October 10, 2025, at KUPE venue in Sofia, Bulgaria. The website serves as the primary marketing and ticketing platform for the event.

## Core Requirements
- **Event Promotion**: Showcase the Turkish Disco Pop DJ party with key details (date, time, venue)
- **Multi-language Support**: Provide content in English, Turkish, Bulgarian, and Arabic
- **Ticket Sales**: Drive traffic to external ticketing platform (tickets.baklavanights.com)
- **SEO Optimization**: Implement comprehensive SEO meta tags, Open Graph, and structured data
- **Analytics Integration**: Track user interactions with Facebook Pixel and Google Analytics
- **Responsive Design**: Ensure optimal viewing experience across all devices
- **Social Media Integration**: Connect with Instagram profiles for event partners
- **Cookie Consent**: Implement GDPR-compliant cookie consent management

## Key Goals
1. **User Acquisition**: Convert visitors into ticket buyers through clear CTAs
2. **Brand Awareness**: Establish Baklava Nights as a premier Turkish music event brand
3. **International Reach**: Cater to diverse audience with multi-language support
4. **Performance**: Ensure fast loading times and smooth user experience
5. **Compliance**: Maintain privacy and data protection standards

## Target Audience
- Turkish music enthusiasts in Bulgaria and surrounding regions
- Nightlife and entertainment seekers in Sofia
- International visitors interested in Turkish culture
- Corporate groups looking for event packages

## Success Metrics
- Ticket sales conversion rate
- Website traffic and engagement metrics
- Social media follower growth
- Multi-language usage statistics
- UTM campaign performance tracking

## Timeline
- **Event Date**: October 10, 2025
- **Website Launch**: ASAP (currently live at baklavanights.com)
- **Ongoing Updates**: Regular content and promotional updates until event date

## Constraints
- Single-page application (no backend server)
- External ticketing system integration
- Multi-language content management within static HTML
- Privacy compliance requirements for EU audience
